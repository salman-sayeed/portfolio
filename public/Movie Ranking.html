The Best Movies According to Salman

My top 10 movies of all time

1. The Batman: The Dark Knight

No doubt! One of the greatest batman movies of all time. Im a Batman fan & this movie is a treat for me.

2. 12 Angry Man

I was skeptical about this one but damn, this is one of the best court room drama movie of all time.

3. Grave of The Fireflies

I started watching this anime movie assuming that it's just like every other anime movie out there. Man whats a rollercoaster ride it was.

4. Nightcrawler

This movie made me a fan of jake gyllenhaal. One of his best work.

5. Inception

This movie introduced me with Christopher Nolan & Leonardo DiCaprio. Best movie for both of them.

6. The Batman (2022)

As I was growing up, Robert Pattinson was very popular among girls. I always hated him maybe because you know how girls likes him. The moment I heard that Robert Pattinson is starring in one of my favourite character of all time, Batman. I was speachless & angry until I saw the trailer. Boy I now understand why girls used to like him. I love him now, we boys love him now. He is now our Batman.

7. Tenet

The cinematography and the action were best. The story flew over my head first time, had to re-watch to understand totally.

8. Forrest Gump

Another movie which introduced to me a great actor, Tom Hanks.

9. The Shawshank's Redemption

Mindblowing movie. Mast watch for everyone.

10. Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind

This movie will always have a speical place in my heart.
